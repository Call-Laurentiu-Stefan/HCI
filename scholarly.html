    Design patterns used:

    • Signpost

      • Locating objects in the user proximity

    • Wayfinding
    • Global Navigation

      • Assures the existence of navigational elements – consistently positioned – to help users to access the most important sections of the application/site (BET button to go straight to betting)

    • Your versus my

      • Labeling items with “My” imitates the user’s point of view

    • Sign-up and registration

      • Collect the bare minimum of information needed that still allows your user to participate in the site
